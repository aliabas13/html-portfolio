The best movies according to Ali

Top 3 movies off all time according to Ali

3 idiots

"3 Idiots" is a popular Bollywood film released in 2009, directed by Rajkumar Hirani. The story follows the adventures of three close friends, Rancho, Farhan, and Raju, who are engineering students at an elite Indian university..

Chichore

"Chhichhore" is a Bollywood film released in 2019. Directed by Nitesh Tiwari, the movie revolves around a group of friends who reunite after many years when one of their children faces a life-threatening situation.

Tare zameen par

"Taare Zameen Par" is a Bollywood film released in 2007, directed by Aamir Khan. The story revolves around a young boy named Ishaan Awasthi, who struggles with dyslexia, a learning disability that makes it difficult for him to read and write.
